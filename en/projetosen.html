Projects

Here are my recent projects:

  • King of Motorcycles:
    King of Motorcycles is a motorcycle sales system developed to simplify the advertisement and negotiation of motorcycles. The platform allows users to view models, prices, and information in a practical and organized way. Its goal is to provide a simple, fast, and modern experience for both customers and sellers.

  • Social Project: The iFrame Project is an application developed to simulate a smartphone interface directly in the browser. The goal of the project is to practice responsiveness and page navigation using HTML, CSS, and iFrames in a practical and interactive way.

  • Cordel Project: The Cordel Project is an application developed to present poems in an interactive and visually appealing way. The goal of the project is to promote literary culture and provide a unique experience for users.
